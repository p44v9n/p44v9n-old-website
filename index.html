Bio

paavan is a close-up magician, who specialises in performing at student balls and other events for young people. When not blowing people's minds, he is studying for a degree in Computer Science & Philosophy at Hertford College, University of Oxford.

See what people have to say about him below.

Hire Paavan

Have you got an event coming up? I'd love to talk to you about working there, no matter what the occasion is. Having a magician performing is the perfect way to break ice and entertain guests.

From performing elegant card magic during champagne receptions at black-tie and white-tie balls in majestic Oxford halls, to chilling out and making miracles happen on a sofa at a house party, I have experience in it all.

Just get in contact and we'll work something out.
