Hi there.

My name is Paavan. I'm a magician.


I specialise in performing at student balls and other events for young people. I have also just finished my undergraduate degree in Computer Science & Philosophy at Hertford College, University of Oxford.


Have you got an event coming up? I'd love to talk to you about haveing me perform there, no matter what the occasion is. Hiring a magician is a great way to break ice and entertain your guests. I can wander between standing or seated guests, performing magic tricks to small groups of people. From performing elegant card magic at champagne receptions, to making miracles happen on a sofa at a house party, I have experience in it all.
